Rob Ensign

Multimedia Student * Content Creator * Heavy Metal & Gaming Enthusiast

About

 Rob Ensign is a first-year student in the Multimedia Program at Lethbridge Polytechnic whose focus is to gain new skills and break into the multimedia industry. Some of his first experiments using digital media dates back to 2000-2003 during his High School years in the form of fan made Anime Music Videos.

 Those projects are what inspired him to originally apply to Lethbridge College's Multimedia Program back in 2003, but ended up not partaking in the program and instead re-entered the workforce, something he first did at the age of 15.

 Since that time, he has experimented with other multimedia creatives such as podcasting and live streaming his gameplay of some of his favorite video games on Twitch and editing and posting content on YouTube. He has an insatiable love for gaming as an artform and live music performances. Heavy metal being his favorite genre of music, having witnessed hundreds of bands perform including bands that are considered “gods” of respected genres among wider communities; Slayer being such an example.

 Having never traveled outside of North America, he has a desire to travel the world and visit places he has always taken an interest in such as Ireland, Scotland, UK, the Nordic regions, Germany and Japan - just to name a few. His hopes and dream are to gain the skills and connections within the industry to have an opportunity to fulfill that traveling desire while also engaging in his interests of music and gaming.
